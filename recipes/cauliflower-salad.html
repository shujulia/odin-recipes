Cauliflower Salad

Chrissy Teigen's Cauliflower Orzo Salad

This roasted cauliflower salad is a hit at potlucks as well as a great meal prep option. Keep the pasta salad and spinach separate until ready to serve to prevent wilting. The pasta can be easily subtituted for another, as well as the dried cherries.

Ingredients

Cauliflower and Orzo

  • Cauliflower
  • Olive oil
  • 3 gloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 cup orzo pasta

Dressing and Salad

  • Olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 cup crumbled feta
  • 2/3 cup dried cherries or cranberries
  • 4 cups baby spinach

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 400 ℉ and boil salted water.
  2. Break cauliflower into florets and toss in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25 min until charred. Let cool.
  3. Cook pasta al dente, 1 min below suggested cook time. Drain and toss with oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, honey, dijon mustard, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  5. Add orzo, cauliflower, red onion, cherries, and feta. Mix until well combined.
  6. Add spinach or leave to the side until ready to serve.
