Cast Iron Cornbread

Perfect Thanksgiving Cornbread

I made these for a thanksgiving potluck and even though I had slightly overbaked it, it was still moist. I gave out the recipe for this afterwards, and converted some cornbread haters!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter melted
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup buttermilk, shake before measuring
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 ℉ and place cast iron skillet into the oven with the butter.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
  3. Make a well in the centre and add buttermilk and eggs. Mix, then add slightly cooled butter until just combined.
  4. Remove pan from oven and pour batter into the hot skillet.
  5. Bake until cornbread begins to brown and a toothpick comes out clean (20-23 min). Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
